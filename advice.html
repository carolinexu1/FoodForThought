Nutritional Advice
Foods to Avoid
  • Sugary beverages
  • Foods with trans fats
    • Peanut butter
    • Crackers
    • Muffins
  • White rice, bread, pasta
  • Fruit flavored yogurts
  • Sugary breakfast cereals
  • Dried fruit
  • Graham crackers
Foods to NOT Avoid
  • Whole grains
    • Brown rice
    • Oatmeal
  • Kale
  • Eggs
  • Seafood
  • Plant-based proteins
    • Beans
    • Nuts
    • Seeds
    • Tofu
